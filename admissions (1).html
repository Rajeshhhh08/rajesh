For admissions to educational institutions in India, particularly for higher education like universities, colleges, and professional courses, you might need to look into exams conducted by other organizations such as:

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE): Conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admissions to undergraduate engineering programs (e.g., B.Tech) in premier institutes like the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs).

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET): Also conducted by NTA, NEET is for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses (MBBS/BDS) in government and private institutions.

Common Admission Test (CAT): Conducted by the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs), CAT is for admission to MBA programs in IIMs and other top business schools.

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE): Conducted jointly by the IISc and IITs, GATE is used for admissions to postgraduate programs (M.Tech/Ph.D.) and for recruitment in government organizations.

National Entrance Examination for Design (NEED): Conducted for admissions to undergraduate and postgraduate design courses in the National Institutes of Design (NID).
