Our History

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is India's central agency responsible for conducting prestigious civil service examinations and appointments to various government services and posts. Established in 1926, the UPSC has a rich legacy of selecting the country's top administrators, diplomats, and officers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to select and recommend the most qualified and capable individuals to serve in key administrative and leadership positions within the Indian government. We aim to uphold the principles of merit, integrity, and fairness in our examination and selection processes.

Our Goals

  • Conducting fair and transparent examinations that assess candidates' knowledge, skills, and aptitude.
  • Identifying and nurturing future leaders who will contribute to the development and progress of the nation.
  • Continuous improvement of examination procedures to ensure relevance and effectiveness.
  • Collaborating with various government bodies to streamline recruitment and personnel selection.
  • Promoting diversity and inclusivity in government services.
